About

I'm a UX designer based in Houston, TX, with over three years of experience, currently working at a small agency specializing in industrial digitization. I’ve designed B2B and consumer products across industries like oil and gas, energy retail, healthcare, and community services.

Skilled in both coding and design, I focus on creating thoughtful, polished products while prioritizing team success. Lately, I’ve been exploring system-aware design approaches that go beyond human-centered design to consider the broader ecosystem of stakeholders.

Colophon

This portfolio is a never-ending work in progress. The current version is probably the fifth iteration or so. It feels like a perpetually unfinished painting—I often look at it and wonder, How can I make this look different or better? I love working with HTML and CSS (Tailwind is my go-to), designing directly in the browser to bring ideas to life. Recently, I’ve been drawing much of my design inspiration from Dead Simple Sites , which curates sites that emphasize simplicity and performance.
