About

I'm a UX designer with over three years of experience, currently working at a small agency specializing in industrial digitization. I've designed B2B enterprise and consumer products across industries like oil and gas, energy retail, medical, and community services. Skilled in both coding and design, I’m always ready to create coded prototypes. My degree in philosophy taught me to think critically and question everything. I'm committed to making thoughtful design choices and delivering polished, high-quality products, always prioritizing the team’s success.

Colophon

This portfolio is a never-ending work in progress. The current version is probably the fifth iteration or so. It feels like a perpetually unfinished painting—I often look at it and wonder, How can I make this look different? I love working with HTML and CSS (Tailwind is my go-to), designing directly in the browser to bring ideas to life. Recently, I’ve been drawing much of my design inspiration from Dead Simple Sites , which curates sites that emphasize simplicity and performance.
